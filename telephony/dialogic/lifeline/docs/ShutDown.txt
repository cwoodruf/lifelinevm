Shutdown process:

1) turn off lifeline: 
   a) change to view mode (keep pressing esc)
   b) press Alt-X
   c) enter password
   d) close window

2) restart computer:
    a) open start menu
    b) select "Restart"
    c) click OK
    d) wait
    e) if the computer doesn't restart  in 60 seconds
       do a warm boot of the computer (press small white
       button on front)

When the lifeline program restarts it may not be visible
on the screen. Look for it on the taskbar. To turn it
back into a window click the button on the taksbar and
press Alt-Enter.
