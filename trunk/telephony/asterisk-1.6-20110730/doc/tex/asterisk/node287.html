Summary

The following are all required for PLC to be used:
  • Enable genericplc in the plc section of codecs.conf
  • Enable (and potentially force) jitter buffers on channels
  • Two channels must be bridged together for PLC to be used (no Meetme or one-legged calls)
  • The audio must be translated between the two channels and must have slin as a step in the translation process.
